Classic Surface Coating Technology is a Pune-based company specializing in high-quality multicolour powder coating and surface treatment solutions.

Under the leadership of Mr. Madhukar R. Mali, the company has established a strong reputation for delivering reliable, durable, and precision-based coating services.

With advanced infrastructure and industry expertise, we enhance the durability, performance, and aesthetic appeal of products across multiple industrial sectors.

Equipped with an oven size of 21 ft × 7 ft × 10.5 ft to efficiently handle both standard and oversized components.

We maintain high standards of quality, timely delivery, and complete customer satisfaction in every project.

We are committed to delivering cost-effective and environmentally responsible solutions while building long-term relationships through trust and excellence.
