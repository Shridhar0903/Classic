OUR SERVICES

High Quality Coating Services

We provide advanced coating solutions for durability, protection and premium finishing.

Powder Coating
(Durable Finishing)

Our powder coating service provides a high-quality, durable, and attractive finish for metal surfaces. It is widely used across industries due to its strong resistance to corrosion, chemicals, and weather conditions.

Sand Blasting
(Surface Preparation)

Sand blasting is an essential pre-treatment process used to clean and prepare surfaces before coating. It removes rust, old paint, oil, and contaminants, ensuring better coating adhesion.

7 Tank Process
(Surface Treatment)

The 7-tank process is a systematic cleaning and chemical treatment method used before powder coating to ensure superior results.
